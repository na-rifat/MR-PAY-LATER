== Description ==

### Mr Pay Later: the Buy Now, Paylater service in Malaysia.

Provide ability to split your payments into 3 easy installment with 0% interest.

Mr Pay Later’s mission is to **Make things affordable to everyone**. Our plugin’s users are e-commerce platform that has WooCommerce integrated.

Mr Pay Later’s plugin will redirect customers to its own platform to perform the transaction and return transaction status back to merchant.

== Installation ==

### INSTALL FROM WITHIN WORDPRESS

1. Visit the plugins page within your dashboard and select ‘Add New’;
1. Search for ‘Mr Pay Later’;
1. Activate Mr Pay Later from your Plugins page;
1. Go to 'after activation’ below.

### INSTALL MANUALLY

1. Upload the ‘wordpress-mrpaylater’ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory;
1. Activate the Mr Pay Later plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress;
1. Go to 'after activation' below.

### AFTER ACTIVATION

1. Enter merchant code that we will be provided when business are verified on Mr Pay Later Platform. (Only verified merchant able to use our services)
1. You should see Mr Pay Later logo during checkout process
1. Go through the process and once complete you will be able to view the status in WooCommerce.
1. You’re done!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Are you regulated? =

We are registered and approved under Payments Network Malaysia(PAYNET).

= How many installment options do you provide? =

Currently we provide the option to pay installment for 2 months or 3 months. We are planning to bring more installment plans very very soon!

= How and when will be the payment deducted? =

How = The payment will be auto deducted from customer bank account via auto debit.

When = The 1st payment will be deducted from customer account within 3 to 4 working days while the next payments will be deducted for subsequent months.

= What if Customer want a refund? =

We follow the refund policy of the respective merchants.

As soon as the merchant approves the refund, we will refund back customer money.

= Are you secured? =

If you are worry about security, worry no more as we are partnered with respective banks.

So the only way for customer to lose their financial information is if somebody hacks the whole bank!

= Do you take any of our financial information? =

No! We don't take any financial information from customer.

The utmost information that we take from customer is only their IC number.
